🍰 Chocolate Cake

Description

Rich, moist, and deeply chocolatey, this cake is perfect for celebrations or whenever you crave a sweet indulgence. Serve it plain or with your favorite frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup hot water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a cake pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Whisk in milk, oil, and egg until smooth.
  4. Stir in hot water; batter will be thin.
  5. Pour into pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Let cool before frosting or serving.
