Garlic Bread Recipe

Description

Garlic bread is a popular side dish made by infusing bread with a delicious mix of garlic, butter, and herbs. Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, it pairs perfectly with pasta, soups, or can even be enjoyed on its own as a snack. This recipe is quick to prepare and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette or Italian bread
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter (softened)
  • 3-4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Grated Parmesan or mozzarella cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the bread in half lengthwise or into thick slices, depending on preference.
  3. In a bowl, mix softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and salt.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the bread slices.
  5. Optional: Sprinkle grated cheese on top for a cheesy garlic bread version.
  6. Place the bread on a baking tray and bake for 10-12 minutes until golden and crispy.
  7. For extra crispiness, broil for 1-2 minutes at the end while watching closely.
  8. Serve warm with pasta, soup, or enjoy as is!
