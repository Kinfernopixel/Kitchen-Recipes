Description

Paneer Tikka Masala is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with grilled paneer cubes simmered in a spiced tomato-based gravy. This vegetarian classic is beloved for its smoky aroma, bold flavors, and comforting texture ‚Äî a perfect companion to naan or basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp each: turmeric, red chili, garam masala, cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil (for grilling)
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 2 pureed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and dry spices into a marinade.
  2. Coat paneer cubes and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Grill or pan-fry paneer until golden and lightly charred.
  4. Saut√© onions until golden; add tomato puree and spices.
  5. Cook until oil separates, then stir in grilled paneer.
  6. Add cream, simmer on low for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
