Description

This simple pasta dish is quick to prepare and endlessly versatile. Pair it with your favorite sauce, add vegetables or protein, and enjoy a comforting meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Salt for boiling water
  • Sauce (tomato, pesto, or Alfredo)
  • Olive oil
  • Optional toppings: grated cheese, fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Boil water with a pinch of salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook until al dente.
  3. Drain and toss with a little olive oil.
  4. Stir in your preferred sauce.
  5. Top with cheese or herbs if desired and serve hot.
