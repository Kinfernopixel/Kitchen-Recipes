Salad Recipe

Description

This fresh garden salad is a light, crisp, and colorful dish packed with nutrients. Featuring a mix of fresh vegetables and a simple homemade dressing, it's the perfect side or even a main dish for those seeking a healthy option. Whether you're enjoying it as a starter or a refreshing lunch, this salad brings a burst of flavor and crunch to every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped or cherry tomatoes halved)
  • 1/2 red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 bell pepper (sliced, any color)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: feta cheese, croutons, or olives

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop or slice the vegetables as listed above.
  3. In a large bowl, combine all the chopped vegetables and greens.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice (or vinegar), salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  6. Top with optional ingredients like feta, olives, or croutons if desired.
  7. Serve immediately for the freshest taste.
