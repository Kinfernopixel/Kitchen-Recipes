Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza is a delicious and customizable dish perfect for any occasion. Whether you prefer a classic Margherita, a meat lover's delight, or a veggie-packed pie, this recipe gives you a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese right from your own oven. It's fun to make and even better to eat!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C)
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough
  4. Sprinkle cheese and add toppings of your choice
  5. Drizzle a little olive oil on top
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly
